What’s covered

Per-Trip Coverage

Coverage activates when a job is accepted and deactivates on completion — no wasted premium, no gaps in protection for riders and gig workers.

Personal Accident for Workers

Real-time PA coverage for riders and gig workers during active jobs — including medical reimbursement, hospitalisation, and permanent disability.

Third-Party Liability

Covers legal liability arising from accidents, property damage, or bodily injury caused by gig workers during delivery or service — protecting the platform and the worker.
