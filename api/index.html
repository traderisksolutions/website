How it works

From integration to go-live in weeks

We manage the insurer relationships, regulatory filings, and claims — you own the customer experience.

1
Define your use case
Tell us your platform model, product type, and target customer. We'll recommend the right coverage structure and distribution model.
2
Agree on product design
We work with insurer partners to structure coverage terms, pricing, and claims triggers that fit your platform's workflow and user expectations.
3
Integrate the API
REST API with sandbox environment. Quote, bind, and cancel policies programmatically — with webhook callbacks for policy events and claims status.
4
Launch & scale
Go live with full MAS-compliant coverage in Singapore. We handle renewals, regulatory reporting, and ongoing product optimisation as you grow.
