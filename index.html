How TRS works

Everything you need to know about getting covered with Trade Risk Solutions.

TRS offers a comprehensive range of products across three categories: Consumers (Masterpiece, Travel, Motor), Commercial (Construction, Foreign Workers Insurance), and Employee Benefits. Every policy is backed by leading carriers including AXA, Chubb, Allianz, and Zurich.
