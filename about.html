Why we’re here

TRS is a licensed independent insurance broker based in Singapore. We work across 18+ carriers to find coverage that fits your actual risk — not the easiest policy to sell.

Since 2009, we’ve placed coverage for listed companies, logistics operators, growing SMEs, and individuals across Singapore. By staying independent, we stay accountable to you.

When a claim comes in, our job is to fight for the outcome you deserve. We stay involved from first notice to final settlement — we don’t disappear when things get difficult.

We’re not tied to any single insurer. Our advisors compare across 18+ carriers and give you the honest answer — even when that means recommending something we make less on.

We explain your coverage, your exclusions, and your gaps before you sign. If a policy won’t hold up in the scenario you’re worried about, you’ll know before you need it.

We handle the paperwork, applications, and renewals so your team doesn’t have to. Complex placements that take weeks elsewhere get done in days — without trading quality for speed.

Most of our clients have been with us for years. We know their business, their risk profile, and when their needs change. Our value compounds the longer we work together.
