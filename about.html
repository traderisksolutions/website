TRS is an AI-native insurance brokerage based in Singapore. With 15+ years of experience, we work across all industries and business sizes — providing the right solutions to protect what you’ve built.

From early-stage startups to Fortune 500 companies, there is always a smarter way to manage your risk exposure. We find the right coverage at the right cost, so you can focus on growing — not on risk.
