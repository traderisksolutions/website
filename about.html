Why we’re here

TRS is a technology-driven insurance brokerage based in Singapore. With over 15 years of deep institutional placement experience, we partner across industries and enterprise scales to engineer the exact risk frameworks required to defend your corporate growth.

From early-stage technology startups to institutional enterprises, we identify a smarter, cleaner path to managing risk exposure. Our teams secure the correct scale of coverage at an optimized price point, allowing you to focus entirely on scaling your core operations.

Our corporate fiduciary duty belongs entirely to you, not the insurance companies. We operate as an aligned extension of your internal finance or operations team, working tirelessly to structure bespoke coverage parameters, negotiate aggressive market premiums, and aggressively defend your corporate interests during complex claims processing.

We maintain total market independence with zero proprietary volume quotas or hidden carrier alignments. By analyzing risk placement structures across a comprehensive network of 18 Singapore-licensed insurance institutions, we ensure that your policy placement is driven entirely by structural coverage superiority and competitive cost efficiency.

Corporate risk should never be hidden behind confusing jargon. We translate dense, highly complex legal policies into transparent risk management summaries, clearly highlighting your actual operational exclusions, internal deductibles, and policy thresholds before any contract signatures occur.

Traditional corporate insurance placement is plagued by slow manual processes. By pairing seasoned risk specialists with custom digital workflows, we eliminate administrative drag to deliver tailored multi-insurer comparative quotes and compliant policy binders within a tight 48-hour window.

As your corporate headcount, regional footprints, and operational complexities evolve, your coverage must scale in parallel. We conduct proactive bi-annual program audits and risk assessment reviews, ensuring your risk architecture remains highly resilient against volatile cross-border regulatory shifts and emerging macroeconomic liabilities.
