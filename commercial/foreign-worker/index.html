WICA Medical Expenses

Covers all work injury-related medical costs as mandated by the Work Injury Compensation Act — including specialist treatment and hospitalisation.

Permanent Disability

Lump sum compensation paid to the worker for partial or total permanent incapacity resulting from a work injury, as required under WICA schedules.

Death Benefit

Compensation payment to the worker's dependants in the event of death arising from a work accident or occupational disease, at the rates set by MOM.

Repatriation Costs

Covers the costs of repatriating an injured foreign worker to their home country for ongoing treatment or upon death. Required under FWI policy conditions.
