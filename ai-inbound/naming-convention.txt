PURPOSE
-------
Files in this folder power the AI that auto-drafts replies to new inbound
enquiries. Keep files focused, factual, and free of binding commitments.
The AI reads these files — clear, plain English works best.


FORMAT
------
[category]-[product-or-topic]-[mmm-yyyy].txt

Rules:
  - Lowercase letters, hyphens and numbers only — no spaces, no underscores
  - Always end with the month-year the file was last updated (e.g. jun-2026)
  - Max 60 characters total
  - Use .txt format (plain text, UTF-8)
  - One topic per file — do not combine unrelated products in one file


CATEGORIES
----------
faq-          Frequently asked questions about a product or topic
product-      Product overview: what it covers, who it's for, key features
process-      Step-by-step procedures: how to get a quote, claims steps
pricing-      Indicative premiums and rating factors (never binding quotes)
objections-   How to respond to common concerns or pushback
support-      Renewals, endorsements, post-sale service information
company-      TRS background, credentials, licences, team, track record

WHAT TO INCLUDE
---------------
  - Plain-language answers to questions a new lead would ask
  - What the product covers (and key exclusions)
  - What information TRS needs to produce a quote
  - Indicative premium ranges (with clear "subject to underwriting" caveat)
  - How claims work (high level, 3–5 steps)
  - Why use TRS over alternatives
  - Company background and MAS licence confirmation


WHAT NOT TO INCLUDE
-------------------
  - Client names, claim amounts, or case-specific details
  - Binding premium quotes or guaranteed rates
  - Underwriter-confidential policy wordings (without compliance sign-off)
  - Internal pricing grids or commission structures
  - Personal opinions or off-the-cuff promises


VERSIONING
----------
When updating an existing file, update the month-year suffix and delete
the old file from Drive (the AI will automatically de-index the removed file
on the next index run).

  Old:  faq-marine-cargo-mar-2026.txt
  New:  faq-marine-cargo-jun-2026.txt   ← upload new, delete old


GETTING FILES INDEXED
---------------------
After uploading a new file to this folder:
1. Go to the TRS dashboard → Analytics → RAG Index → Inbound AI tab
2. Click "Re-index New" to pick up the new file
3. Confirm the filename appears in the indexed files list

The AI will use the new file immediately for the next auto-draft.
