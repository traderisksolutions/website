Health coverage for every employee

Group Medical Insurance

Hospitalisation, surgical, and major medical coverage for all employees. Includes Ward B1 or higher, pre- and post-hospitalisation benefits, and optional MediShield Life integration.

Outpatient GP Coverage

Unlimited or capped GP visits at a network of panel clinics island-wide. Includes basic diagnostics, prescribed medication, and referrals — with no out-of-pocket costs for employees.

Dental Benefits

Preventive (scaling and polishing) and basic restorative dental treatment. Optional enhancement to include orthodontics and complex dental procedures.

Specialist & Wellness

Specialist consultation, diagnostic imaging, and optional wellness rider covering health screenings, vaccinations, and optical benefits.
