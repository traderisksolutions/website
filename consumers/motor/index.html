Motor insurance in Singapore covers third-party liability (mandatory by law), and optionally own damage from accidents, fire, theft, and personal accident benefits for driver and passengers.

Premiums are based on your vehicle type, engine capacity, your age and driving experience, no-claims discount (NCD), and claims history. TRS compares rates across 18+ insurers to find the best rate.

No Claims Discount (NCD) is a reduction in your premium for each claim-free year. It starts at 10% after year one and can reach up to 50% after five claim-free years. TRS helps you protect it.
