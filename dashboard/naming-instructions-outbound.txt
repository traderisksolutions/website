TRS AI Knowledge Base — Outbound Campaign File Naming Convention
Updated: Jun 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT THIS FOLDER IS FOR (READ THIS FIRST — FOR THE AI AGENT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
You are the TRS Outbound Campaign AI.
This folder contains your knowledge base. When drafting cold outreach emails
for a campaign, you retrieve the most relevant documents from this folder to
write accurate, compelling, product-specific emails on behalf of TRS.

This folder is for COLD OUTREACH only — prospect-facing campaign emails
sent via Instantly. It is NOT for replying to inbound enquiries (that is
the engagement_ai_agent folder).

Use these documents to:
  - Describe TRS's insurance products clearly and accurately
  - Quote indicative pricing or coverage limits when relevant
  - Reference case studies or client outcomes as social proof
  - Establish TRS's credentials and why a prospect should respond
  - Match the email angle to the product focus of the campaign

Do NOT fabricate coverage terms, premiums, or statistics. Only cite what
is in the retrieved passages. If the documents do not support a specific
claim, omit it.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FILE NAMING CONVENTION (FOR HUMANS UPLOADING DOCUMENTS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FORMAT
  [topic]-[type]-[mmm-yyyy].pdf

FIELDS
  topic   → the insurance line (see list below)
  type    → what the document is (see list below)
  mmm     → 3-letter month abbreviation, lowercase (jan feb mar apr may jun
             jul aug sep oct nov dec)
  yyyy    → 4-digit year

TOPIC LIST
  marine        construction    cargo         property
  fire          liability       motor         travel
  engineering   hull            benefits      employee
  general       company

TYPE LIST
  pricing          → indicative premiums, rates, coverage limits
  policy-wording   → what is and isn't covered, key exclusions
  underwriting     → underwriting criteria, risk appetite
  guide            → plain-language explanation of the product
  schedule         → policy schedule or coverage summary table
  endorsement      → additional covers or riders available
  case-study       → client story, outcome, sector relevance
  credentials      → TRS licences, awards, track record, about us

EXAMPLES
  marine-pricing-may-2026.pdf
  benefits-underwriting-jan-2026.pdf
  construction-policy-wording-mar-2026.pdf
  property-schedule-feb-2026.pdf
  motor-guide-apr-2026.pdf
  liability-case-study-jun-2026.pdf
  company-credentials-jan-2026.pdf

RULES
  - Lowercase only, hyphens between all words, no spaces
  - Always end with .pdf
  - One document per file — do not bundle multiple products
  - When a document is updated, upload the new version with the current
    month-year (do not overwrite the old file — the AI retrieves the most
    relevant version based on the campaign context)
  - .txt files in this folder ARE read by the AI — use them to provide
    instructions or context (like this file)
  - Keep each file under 20 pages for best retrieval accuracy
  - The AI retrieves up to 6 passages per campaign email — keep documents
    focused so the right passage ranks highest
