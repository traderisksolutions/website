────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT THIS FOLDER IS FOR (READ THIS FIRST — FOR THE AI AGENT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
You are Agent 2 — the TRS Engagement AI.
This folder contains your knowledge base. When a lead replies to an email,
you retrieve the most relevant documents from this folder and use them to
draft accurate, on-brand replies on behalf of TRS Account Executives.

Use these documents to:
  - Answer questions about coverage, policy terms, and what TRS offers
  - Handle objections and pricing pushback professionally
  - Explain the claims process clearly and without alarming the client
  - Provide TRS company credentials when the lead asks about who we are
  - Match responses to the client's specific insurance need

Do NOT fabricate terms, figures, or procedures. Only cite what is in the
retrieved passages. If the documents do not answer the client's question,
say TRS will revert within 2 business days.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FILE NAMING CONVENTION (FOR HUMANS UPLOADING DOCUMENTS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FORMAT
  [topic]-[type]-[mmm-yyyy].pdf

FIELDS
  topic   → the insurance line or subject area (see list below)
  type    → what the document is (see list below)
  mmm     → 3-letter month abbreviation, lowercase (jan feb mar apr may jun
             jul aug sep oct nov dec)
  yyyy    → 4-digit year

TOPIC LIST
  marine        construction    cargo         property
  fire          liability       motor         travel
  engineering   hull            benefits      employee
  claims        general         company

TYPE LIST
  faq            → frequently asked questions about coverage or process
  objection      → how to handle pushback (pricing, competitor, delay)
  process        → step-by-step procedures (claims, renewal, onboarding)
  credentials    → TRS company background, licences, awards, track record
  coverage       → what a specific policy covers and excludes
  pricing        → indicative rates, premium ranges, discount conditions
  guide          → general explanations for the client (plain-language)

EXAMPLES
  general-faq-jun-2026.pdf
  claims-process-mar-2026.pdf
  company-credentials-jan-2026.pdf
  liability-objection-may-2026.pdf
  motor-coverage-apr-2026.pdf
  benefits-pricing-feb-2026.pdf
  marine-guide-jun-2026.pdf

RULES
  - Lowercase only, hyphens between all words, no spaces
  - Always end with .pdf
  - One document per file — do not bundle multiple products or topics
  - When a document is updated, upload the new version with the current
    month-year (do not overwrite the old file — the AI loads the most
    relevant version based on the client's query)
  - .txt files in this folder ARE read by the AI — use them to provide
    instructions or context (like this file)
  - Keep each file under 20 pages for best retrieval accuracy
  - The AI retrieves up to 6 passages per query — keep documents focused
    so the right passage ranks highest
