What’s covered

Freight Forwarder Liability

Covers freight forwarders against cargo loss or damage claims arising from their role as carrier, agent, or bailee under FIATA or standard trading conditions.

Warehouse Legal Liability

Protects warehouse operators against claims for loss or damage to goods held in custody — whether as bailee or under a formal storage agreement.

Supply Chain Disruption

Business interruption cover triggered by disruptions at key nodes in your supply chain — suppliers, ports, or logistics hubs — with or without direct physical damage.

Marine Cargo Open Cover

Blanket marine cargo policy for logistics operators moving goods on behalf of clients — all shipments automatically covered as they are declared.
